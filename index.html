I am a PhD student under the supervision of Prof. Masashi Sugiyama in Sugiyama-Honda-Yokoya laboratory, Department of computer science, The University of Tokyo.

Research interests: I am interested in machine learning algorithm analysis and design in general. More specifically, my current research topics include:

  • Loss function
  • Learning with reject option
  • Weakly-supervised learning
  • Domain adaptation
